Social Media Campaign

A multi-platform content campaign that grew an audience by 40% over three months.

Role Content Designer
Year 2024
Tools Photoshop, Premiere Pro

This campaign was designed to revitalise a brand's social media presence across Instagram and TikTok. The scope included a new visual content strategy, templated post designs, short-form video concepts, and a three-month content calendar that drove measurable growth in engagement and followers.

The Brief

The client's social channels had stagnated — inconsistent visuals and irregular posting had led to declining engagement. The goal was to establish a recognisable aesthetic, create reusable design templates that their internal team could maintain, and produce a batch of launch content to kick off the new direction.

Creative Approach

The visual system centred on a restricted palette and consistent typographic treatment, creating instant recognisability in a crowded feed. Each post format — product highlights, quotes, behind-the-scenes, and announcements — was given its own template structure while remaining visually unified.

Short-form video scripts were written around trending audio and native platform behaviours, keeping production simple enough for the client to continue independently after handoff.

Results

Over the three-month campaign, the client's combined following grew by 40%, with average post engagement increasing by over 60%. The templated system was successfully handed off and the client's team continued publishing consistently beyond the campaign period.
