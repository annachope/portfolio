Tabsi

A video and motion graphics campaign promoting Tabsi, an AI-powered candidate tracker — translating a hiring tool into clear, energetic, watchable content.

Role Creative Director, Video Editor, Client Liaison
Year 2025
Tools Premiere Pro, After Effects

I led the creative direction and editing for Tabsi's launch video, shaping the narrative and motion language while serving as the main point of contact with the client. The goal was to make an AI candidate tracker feel approachable and exciting — not just another piece of HR software.

The Problem

Tabsi needed to explain a fairly technical product — AI-assisted candidate tracking — to busy hiring teams in under a minute. Recruiting tools tend to be marketed with dry feature lists and stock office footage, which buries what actually makes a product worth trying. The brief was to cut through that and give Tabsi a voice that felt modern, confident, and human.

The Solution

I built the story around a single clear promise — find the right candidate faster — and let motion graphics carry the explanation. Animated UI moments, kinetic type, and a tight pacing rhythm turn abstract features into something you can follow at a glance.

Throughout production I worked directly with the client to align on tone, review cuts, and fold in feedback, keeping the edit on-brand while protecting the pacing and clarity of the final piece.

The Outcome

The result is a launch video that gives Tabsi a distinct, energetic identity and explains the product without losing momentum. The motion system established here is reusable across future clips, social cut-downs, and product updates, giving the brand a consistent visual voice to grow into.
