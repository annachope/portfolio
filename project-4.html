Design by Chope

A self-started Etsy shop specializing in music-inspired posters that celebrate iconic artists.

Role Illustrator & Shop Owner
Year 2022 – Present
Tools Photoshop, Illustrator

Design by Chope is my own Etsy shop creating music-inspired poster art that celebrates iconic artists and albums. The work fosters a connection with a vibrant community of music lovers, blending art and passion to inspire and resonate with fellow fans.

  Visit the Etsy Shop   Instagram

The Shop

Each poster reinterprets an artist or album through bold type, color, and texture — from psychedelic liquid lettering and glitchy collage to clean, retro-inspired layouts. The range lets me explore a different visual language for every artist while keeping a consistent, fan-first point of view.
