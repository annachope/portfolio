Editorial Layout Design

A print and digital magazine spread exploring the intersection of art and technology.

Role Editorial Designer
Year 2023
Tools InDesign, Illustrator

A ten-page editorial spread commissioned for a design publication's annual technology issue. The layout needed to balance dense written content with bold visual impact, creating a reading experience that felt dynamic across both print and its digital PDF counterpart.

The Brief

The publication wanted a feature spread that pushed their usual grid further — something that felt experimental without sacrificing readability. The subject matter, exploring how generative AI is changing creative practice, gave room to play with fragmented and layered compositions that echoed the theme.

Layout Approach

The grid system used a flexible 12-column baseline that allowed for both structured multi-column text and full-width visual moments. Pull quotes were treated as graphic elements in their own right, using large-scale type and tight leading to create contrast against the body text density.

Colour was used sparingly — a single accent hue carried through headings, captions, and decorative rules to create cohesion without overwhelming the photography.

Outcome

The spread was published in the print edition and featured as a highlight in the publication's digital issue. The editor noted it as one of the most visually distinct layouts in the issue, and it has since been referenced in the publication's internal style guidelines as an example of experimental grid use.
