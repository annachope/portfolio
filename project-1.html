Switch to FriendliAI

A high-conversion landing page for a competitive acquisition campaign targeting high-spend AI inference users — driving migration to FriendliAI through a $50K inference credit incentive.

Role Landing Page Designer
Year 2025
Tools Webflow

I designed and built a conversion-focused landing page for FriendliAI's acquisition campaign, aimed at developers and teams with substantial AI inference spend. The page pairs clear value messaging with a $50K inference-credit incentive to make switching an easy decision.

The Goal

The campaign targeted high-spend inference users and needed to communicate better performance and lower costs — with minimal changes to a team's existing stack. Leadership and I aligned on messaging in shared documents before I built the page in Webflow, working closely within FriendliAI's brand guidelines.

From Copy to Components

Starting from a written content outline, I translated the messaging into a structured page — a bold hero, focused value props, a "what you get" breakdown, and a simple three-step migration flow. The layout stays clean and keeps the primary call to action, "Apply Now," prominent throughout the scroll.

Outcome

The result is a focused, on-brand page that frames the offer around tangible developer value: better performance, lower cost, and a frictionless switch — all reinforced by the $50K credit incentive.
