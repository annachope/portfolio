Brand Identity Design

A complete visual identity system for a local boutique — from logo to brand guidelines.

Role Brand Designer
Year 2024
Tools Illustrator, Photoshop

This project involved developing a full brand identity for a local boutique looking to establish a refined, modern aesthetic. The work covered logo design, typography selection, colour palette, and a complete set of brand guidelines to ensure consistency across all touchpoints.

The Brief

The client wanted a brand that felt elevated and contemporary while remaining approachable. The identity needed to work across print materials, social media, signage, and packaging. The primary challenge was balancing a clean, minimal look with enough warmth to reflect the boutique's personal touch.

Design Process

The process began with an in-depth discovery session to understand the client's vision, target audience, and competitive landscape. From there, I developed three distinct concept directions before narrowing down to the final identity through a collaborative review process.

The final logo mark draws on geometric simplicity, pairing a custom wordmark with a minimal icon that works as a standalone stamp for packaging and tags. The type system uses a high-contrast serif for headings alongside a clean geometric sans-serif for body copy.

Outcome

The final brand system was delivered as a comprehensive guidelines document alongside all production-ready asset files. The boutique launched with the new identity to positive reception, noting an immediate lift in perceived quality and brand recognition among their customer base.
