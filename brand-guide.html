Brand Guidelines

The visual system behind the portfolio — a deep olive foundation, a single electric green for emphasis, soft periwinkle for contrast, hairline structure, sharp edges, and restrained motion. Built to feel technical, confident, and quietly playful.

Logo / Wordmark

Anna Chope.

The wordmark is set in Space Grotesk, 600–700 weight, with tight letter-spacing. It always appears in near-white on the ink background, or ink on green. Give it room — never crowd it, stretch it, or apply gradients or shadows.

Color Palette

Ink #272A1D Primary background. The warm olive-black foundation of every page.
Green #46F177 Primary accent. CTAs, links, highlights, active states. Use sparingly for punch.
Moss #435034 Supporting tone. Offset frames, depth, and grounded green moments.
Periwinkle #AEBAF9 Secondary accent. Soft counterpoint to the green — subheads, tags, detail.
White #FFFFFF Text (as near-white #F4F5EF) and type on green or light surfaces.
Black #000000 Reserved for maximum-contrast type and print where ink is too soft.

Card Component

Hairline + surface

Cards sit on a subtly lifted surface with a 1px hairline border. On hover, the border turns green and the card lifts — never rounds.

Principles & Motion

Sharp edges

No rounded corners. Every surface, button, and image is squared off for a crisp, architectural feel.

Hairline structure

Thin 1px lines — not heavy boxes — define space, divide sections, and frame content.

Restrained motion

Text rotates in the hero, content reveals on scroll, and accents respond on hover — always subtle, never busy.

One loud color

Green does the talking. It marks the single most important thing in any view; everything else stays calm.
